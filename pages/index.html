About me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio ! I’m Eddy, a french student in video game design and project management passionate about game creation. Through the projects I’ve done, I’ve been working with graphists, designers, sound designers and I learned how to manage a production and communicate with a team.

As a player, I really enjoy games with a strong narrative such as Beyond Two Souls, Grim Fandango and the Dark Souls Series. I am always eager to play with my friends on my Nintendo Switch and having more personal experiences with games like Hollow Knight and Bioshock.

Looking forward to meet you and work with you !
